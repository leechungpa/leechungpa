Chungpa Lee is a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at Yonsei University, where he is jointly advised by Professors [Joseph H.T. Kim](https://yonsei.elsevierpure.com/en/persons/joseph-ht-kim/) (Statistics) and [Jy-yong Sohn](https://itml.yonsei.ac.kr/professor/) (Machine Learning), and worked closely with Professor [Kibok Lee](https://ml.yonsei.ac.kr/professor). Early in his Ph.D., he spent two years at an early-stage startup led by Professors [Jongho Im](https://www.yd3sl.com/#people) and Joseph Kim. From 2025 to 2026, he was a visiting researcher at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, working with Professor [Kangwook Lee](https://kangwooklee.com/). Beginning in August 2026, he will be a student intern at the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Washington, working with Professor [Sewoong Oh](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~sewoong/).
